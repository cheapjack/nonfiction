The Arcade De Bruno

Inspired by the evolution of Text Adventures and 8-Bit computer games The Arcade de Bruno, is a way of playing games that illustrate biological mechanisms that are established and falsified enough to become the Black Boxes Bruno Latour talks about; established ready-made science much in common with the kits I'm thinking about.

Use the red and green buttons to tab through links and white to select and the black button to return here. blue and yellow move up and down through links

Black Box Phage is a game made in html5 & bitsy showing how Bacteriophage interact with cells. This Black Box has helped humans to end things like Tuberculosis and Smallpox and without it the structure of DNA may never have been found.
