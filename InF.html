Interactive Non-Fiction Arcade

Inspired by the evolution of Text Adventures into Interactive Fiction, Interactive Non-Fiction Arcade, is a way of playing text based games that use data from the Internet in a social way like the arcades of the past. Use the red and green buttons to tab through links and white to select and the black button to return here. blue and yellow move up and down through links

Play The Games

Glenn Boulter's game, Reduced To Clear uses data from the Office of National Statistics while Hwa Young Jung's game is like a wiki for British Shopping. or play our older mystery game, The Wray Castle Text Adventure
